This all happened because of the faculty in the MSIT. They were always helpful for me when ever I was in need. The grading system is also good. The infrastucture is also too good. We sit in AC class rooms and 24x7 WI-FI facility.

Coming to the hotel experience, the girls hostel is very good and we have LAN fecility in the hostel rooms to work in the room also. The mess is also very good. We have 4 messes and depending on out taste we can go to the mess that we like. However, we need to register in the mess portal if we want to change the mess.

We participate in many events such as alumini meets where we will get to the skills we need to develop. We participate in each and every IIIT event such as felicity. We had secret santa game as a part of chirsmas.

At present I am learning app developmet. It is very exciting to learn about version control and development of web pages.
